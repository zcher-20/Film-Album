# Film Album

A scroll-driven digital photo album with a film-print aesthetic — photos glide through a continuous carousel as you scroll, each with editable captions and a film-strip timeline.

## Features

- **Scroll-linked motion** — photos flow continuously through the carousel as you scroll, sized and faded by depth
- **Editable captions** — click any caption to rename it; saves automatically
- **Add & delete photos** — manage your album right in the browser (＋ to add, ↻ to reset to the originals)
- **Film-strip timeline** — a tick-mark scrubber showing the month and year of each photo
- **Persistent storage** — edits, additions, and deletions are saved locally via IndexedDB

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any modern browser, or visit the live site (if GitHub Pages is enabled).

Scroll to move through the photos. Use the arrow buttons, dots, or arrow keys to jump between them.

## Notes

Photos and edits are stored in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB), so they stay on your own device and don't sync across browsers or visitors.
